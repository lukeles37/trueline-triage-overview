Emergency — Call 911 or Proceed to Your Local ER
Chest pain · Respiratory distress · Altered consciousness · Active hemorrhage · Hemodynamic instability · Any life-threatening presentation — call 911 immediately or proceed directly to your nearest Emergency Room. TrueLine clinical staff will assist with triage support while emergency services are contacted.
