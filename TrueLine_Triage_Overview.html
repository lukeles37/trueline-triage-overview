# TrueLine Health — Clinical Triage Framework ### Interactive Three-Pillar Model Overview > **Confidential — Internal Use Only** > TrueLine Health Systems · 2026 --- ## Overview This repository contains the interactive clinical triage reference guide for TrueLine's **Three-Pillar Clinical Model** — the structured framework governing how patients are assessed, routed, and managed across TrueLine's integrated employer health teams in New Brunswick and Nova Scotia. Open `TrueLine_Triage_Overview.html` in any modern browser. No server, build step, or dependencies required — it is fully self-contained. **Live preview:** [TrueLine Triage Overview](https://htmlpreview.github.io/?https://github.com/lukeles37/trueline-triage-overview/blob/main/TrueLine_Triage_Overview.html) --- ## The Three-Pillar Model | Pillar | Roles | Access | Function | |--------|-------|--------|----------| | **Pillar 1 — Primary Care Intake** | Pharmacist · RPT · LPN | 24–36 hrs · In-person | Patient entry point. Pharmacist leads clinical assessment and prescribing. RPT delivers injection services, technical Rx counselling, and medication preparation. LPN conducts structured intake — vitals, health history, and initial assessment — under pharmacist supervision. | | **Pillar 2 — Resolutions and Referrals** | LPN (continuing) · NP · PA | 48–72 hrs · Virtual/Phone/In-person | Escalation pathway for cases exceeding Pillar 1 scope. LPN continues in clinical support under NP supervision. NP provides autonomous diagnosis, full prescribing authority, and serves as CPA Authorizing Partner. PA assesses, resolves, and refers within PA delegated scope — and spans into Pillar 3 for complex cases. | | **Pillar 3 — Advanced and Emergency Care** | ER Physician · PA (continuing) | Initiated <24 hrs · Appt 3–5 business days | Complex, unstable, and high-acuity cases. ER Physician holds full CPA authorization authority. PA continues from Pillar 2 under ER Physician supervision for cases requiring delegated advanced clinical management. | **Emergency presentations** (chest pain, respiratory distress, altered consciousness, active hemorrhage, hemodynamic instability) bypass all pillars — **call 911 or proceed to the nearest ER immediately.** --- ## Dual-Pillar Roles Two roles span adjacent pillars — mirroring how clinical acuity flows naturally rather than resetting at each handoff: - **LPN** — Structured intake in Pillar 1 (vitals, health history, assessment under pharmacist supervision) → continuing clinical support in Pillar 2 (under NP supervision) - **PA** — Assessment, resolution, and referral in Pillar 2 (within PA delegated scope) → complex case management in Pillar 3 (under ER Physician supervision) --- ## Interactive Features The guide includes three tabs: ### Three Pillars - Visual display of all three pillars and their constituent roles - Click any role card to expand a full detail panel showing: - Scope of practice - Prescribing and clinical authority - CPA role (where applicable) - Province-specific regulatory notes (NB / NS) - **Province toggle** (Both / NB / NS) filters regulatory notes in real time - Dual-pillar roles (LPN, PA) display cards in both pillars with a "Spans" badge ### Patient Flow - SVG swimlane diagram showing the full triage decision path - Covers: patient arrival → emergency check → RPT + LPN intake → Pharmacist assessment → PA/NP escalation → ER Physician → resolution outcomes - Emergency 911/ER bypass clearly indicated ### Regulatory Matrix - Full comparison table for all 7 roles - Columns: Pillar · Role · NB College/Act · NS College/Act · Prescribing Authority · CPA Participation --- ## Regulatory Scope Summary All TrueLine clinicians are registered under applicable provincial colleges.
