### Interactive Three-Pillar Model Overview > **Confidential — Internal Use Only** > TrueLine Health Systems · 2026 --- ## Overview This repository contains the interactive clinical triage reference guide for TrueLine's **Three-Pillar Clinical Model** — the structured framework governing how patients are assessed, routed, and managed across TrueLine's integrated employer health teams in New Brunswick and Nova Scotia. Open `TrueLine_Triage_Overview.html` in any modern browser. No server, build step, or dependencies required — it is fully self-contained. --- ## The Three-Pillar Model | Pillar | Roles | Function | |--------|-------|----------| | **Pillar 1 — Pharmacy Resources** | Pharmacist · RPT | Patient entry point. Manages minor ailments, medication review, CPA-expanded chronic disease care. | | **Pillar 2 — Nurses** | LPN · RN · NP | Escalation pathway for cases exceeding pharmacist scope. NP provides autonomous diagnosis and prescribing. NP may also serve as CPA Authorizing Partner. | | **Pillar 3 — Physicians** | ER Physician · PA | Complex, unstable, and high-acuity cases. CPA authorization authority. Emergency backstop for all pillars. | **Emergency presentations** (chest pain, respiratory distress, altered consciousness, active hemorrhage, hemodynamic instability) bypass all pillars — **call 911 or proceed to the nearest ER immediately.** --- ## Interactive Features The guide includes three tabs: ### Three Pillars - Visual display of all three pillars and their constituent roles - Click any role card to expand a full detail panel showing: - Scope of practice - Prescribing and clinical authority - CPA role (where applicable) - Province-specific regulatory notes (NB / NS) - **Province toggle** (Both / NB / NS) filters regulatory notes in real time ### Patient Flow - SVG swimlane diagram showing the full triage decision path - Covers: patient arrival → emergency check → RPT intake → Pharmacist assessment → escalation to Nurses → escalation to Physicians → resolution outcomes - Emergency 911/ER bypass clearly indicated ### Regulatory Matrix - Full comparison table for all 7 roles - Columns: Pillar · Role · NB College/Act · NS College/Act · Prescribing Authority · CPA Participation --- ## Regulatory Scope Summary All TrueLine clinicians are registered under applicable provincial colleges.
